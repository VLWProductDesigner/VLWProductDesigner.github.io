Surface View

Surface View are an online e-commerce website. They specialise in bringing a range of print products such as canvases, murals, blinds epic posters and more on a scale that can be seen in and around the home, office or unique personal space. The Surface View brand is aimed at high end market consumers with a love of design and or a keen eye for beautiful imagery.

The challenge for the design team I worked with at Engage was to create a user experience that would allow the customer to choose an image that could be applied across the Surface View product range and tailored to suit the needs of the customer . Part of the task would be to display a high end visual user experience through well thought layout and assets.

The brief presented to me was to assist the design team in creating assets for the website, content management, imagery cropping and resizing, participating in the product shoots and user research. The assets were to be done to a pixel perfect high standard that displayed beautifully with in the design of the website.

Working on this project was both enlightening and inspiring. It enhanced my skill set and design values. It gave perfect opportunity to learn and work with in a great team that both encouraged and displayed great high-end user interface design.
